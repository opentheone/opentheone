# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## 我们的承诺 / Our Pledge

为了营造一个开放、友好的社区，作为贡献者和维护者，我们承诺：让每一位
参与 OpenTheOne 项目的人都能获得无骚扰的体验，无论年龄、体型、可见或不
可见的残疾、种族、性别特征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会
经济地位、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教信仰、性取向。

We pledge to make participation in OpenTheOne a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## 我们的准则 / Our Standards

有助于建设积极环境的行为包括 / Examples of behavior that contributes to a
positive environment include:

- 使用友好和包容的语言 / Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- 尊重不同的观点和经验 / Being respectful of differing viewpoints and
  experiences.
- 优雅地接受建设性批评 / Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- 关注对社区最有利的事情 / Focusing on what is best for the community.
- 对其他社区成员表示同理心 / Showing empathy towards other community
  members.

不可接受的行为包括 / Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- 使用性化语言或图像，以及不受欢迎的性关注或挑逗 / The use of sexualized
  language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- 挑衅、侮辱性／贬低性评论，以及人身或政治攻击 / Trolling, insulting or
  derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- 公开或私下的骚扰 / Public or private harassment.
- 未经明确许可，发布他人的私人信息（如住址、邮箱）/ Publishing others'
  private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without
  explicit permission.
- 其他在专业场合中合理认为不当的行为 / Other conduct which could
  reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## 执行 / Enforcement

不当行为可以通过项目的 [Security 私下渠道](./SECURITY.md) 联系维护者。
所有投诉都会被认真审查与调查，并以维护者认为必要且合适的方式回应。
维护者有义务对事件举报者的身份保密。

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported via the private channels listed in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a
response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
The maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to
the reporter of an incident.
